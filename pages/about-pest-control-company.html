About Us

A Nano-Technology Lab That Solves Pest Problems

Since 2012 we have been protecting Chiang Mai homes and businesses with bio-technology that eliminates pests without poisoning the environment.

Our Story

Started in Chiang Mai. Built for Chiang Mai.

Green Nano Thai was founded in Chiang Mai in 2012 with one clear conviction: that the pest control industry had been using the wrong tools for decades. Synthetic chemical sprays were effective at killing pests - but they also left toxic residue in the homes of the people they were meant to protect. We set out to engineer a better approach, one rooted in plant science rather than petrochemicals.

Over twelve years, we developed and refined our proprietary herbal nano-particle technology - formulations that work at a molecular scale, penetrating voids and timber that conventional treatments cannot reach. Today we protect more than 500 properties across Chiang Mai, from family homes to hotels to commercial kitchens. Our clients choose us because they want pest control that works completely, without compromise on the safety of their family or the environment.
